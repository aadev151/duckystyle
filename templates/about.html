DFT

DFT stands for "Duck Functional Token"


What can you do with a DFT? That's a good question. Well, for instance...
Buy one and enjoy the ownership of a unique picture;
Make a profile picture, just like Eminem did;
Retail one on our website and earn real cash.
And that's actually just a few of the things that you can do with a DFT.




Why should I?

That's just a good way to invest your money! You can be sure that you won't lose your dollars because DuckCoins is a stable currency, always equal to $1.
Moreover, you need no investments or even credit card to start! Just sign up on our website!




How can I start?

After registering on the website, you'll get 10 DuckCoins. You can also earn some more by watching ads. On them, you can buy a DFT from our collection and then retail it, making some money.
Have a good luck!
